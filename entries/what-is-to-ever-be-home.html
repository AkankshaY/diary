What is to ever be home?

I have found a new home And it's been a few weeks Few weeks into Breathing in this space, I have cozied a bit And moved the furniture around. But a strange feeling lingers. The thing is: I don't know how to place myself In a space I didn't create, And call it home, In an alien land, All by myself and My foolish dreams, Even my desk too small to lay bare All my thoughts.
