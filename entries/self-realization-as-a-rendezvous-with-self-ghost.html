Self-realization as a rendezvous with self-ghost

Those moments when I felt most alive, I remember feeling paralyzed, as if struck by lightning in the middle of a storm of unnatural events that conjured around me all of a sudden. I like to imagine that in that split second, I was nudged to pause and smile back at someone I had known but long forgotten.
