Fearless hour

The lights are dim, the hour late, And you have followed me into this hour When my words are loose and unkept Not with the slightest inclination to converse The lights are dim, the hour late, So I will smear any makeup and dance loud and alone on the thin line Ugly, mad, sane and beautiful All the same
