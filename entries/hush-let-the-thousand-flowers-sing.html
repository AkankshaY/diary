Hush, let the thousand flowers sing

Learning to hold words with a little extra room for delight, to let the thousand flowers, sing more profoundly in them In such a way as to not impose my rendition of melody or joy or anything mine I think it can bring a certain sadness in branding your joy as something that only you can afford When it's easy and bad enough already to be convinced that joy comes in recognizably dogmatic shades of red, blue and green
