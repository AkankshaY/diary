Healthy obsessions as deep relationships

I carry my work with me everywhere I go, into my daydreams, and into the night ones. The way that happened, I think, is it just chose to follow me everywhere, and I realized how beautiful a company it is on some days and how annoying it can be on others - either way I'd get really lonely if I were to get rid of it by closing the door on it at 5pm, so that thought eludes me entirely
