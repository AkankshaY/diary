Rebel and revel in narratives
---
Writing as a means to
indulge in alternate personas
rebel against rhetoric narratives
not conform to off the shelf labels

For there's fun in being able to harness a neurodivergent individual, scarred with childhood trauma, into a flamboyantly depressed (no less) manic alter ego, who hates how people lacking any patience to grasp, confidently make a speech out of nothing, riling her up to turn her back and walk away, with a song in her head, mid-conversation.
So I could excuse myself if that thought did ever cross my mind
